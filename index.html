What is a Factorial?

The factorial of a number n (written as n!) is the product of all positive integers from 1 to n.

Example: 5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120

What is a Permutation?

A permutation (nPr) is the number of ways to arrange r items out of n distinct items.

Formula: nPr = n! / (n - r)!

What is a Combination?

A combination (nCr) is the number of ways to select r items from n distinct items without regard to order.

Formula: nCr = n! / (r! × (n - r)!)
