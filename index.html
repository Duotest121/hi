Factorial Machine

Factorial (n!): Multiply all numbers from 1 to n.
Example: 5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120

Permutations (nPr)

Permutations: Arrangements where order matters.
Formula: nPr = n! / (n - r)!

Combinations (nCr)

Combinations: Selections where order doesn’t matter.
Formula: nCr = n! / [r! × (n - r)!]
